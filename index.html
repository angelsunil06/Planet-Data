🌌 Planet Highlights

⚪ Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun. It has no natural moons and experiences extreme temperature variations.

🟡 Venus

Venus is the hottest planet in the Solar System due to its dense carbon dioxide atmosphere and powerful greenhouse effect.

🌍 Earth

Earth is the only known planet that supports life and contains vast oceans, a breathable atmosphere, and diverse ecosystems.

🔴 Mars

Known as the Red Planet, Mars contains the largest volcano and canyon in the Solar System and is a major target for future human exploration.

🟠 Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System and is famous for its Great Red Spot, a giant storm larger than Earth.

🪐 Saturn

Saturn is renowned for its spectacular ring system made of ice, rock, and dust particles.

🔵 Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side, making it unique among planets. Its blue-green color comes from methane in its atmosphere.

🔹 Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and experiences some of the strongest winds in the Solar System.
